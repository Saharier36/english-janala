Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website? +

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use? +

Yes, our platform is completely free to use. You can access all lessons and exercises without any subscription or payment.

3. Do I need to create an account? +

No, you don't necessarily need an account to start learning. However, creating an account will help you track your progress and save your achievements.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary? +

You can build your vocabulary through our interactive vocabulary lessons, daily word challenges, and example sentences with contextual usage.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses? +

Yes, upon completing our structured courses, you will receive a certificate of completion that you can download and share with others.
